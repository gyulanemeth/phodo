PhoDo

The Coolest Photo based ToDo app for Visual Thinkers

People usually forget about things.

That is the reason why we usually write to-do lists. Our brains just don't work like computers, we need reminders to accomplish everything we want.

Imagine the situation that you know that you should call your mother, since it has been a long-long time since you haven't spoken. I don't know about you, but I feel very, very devastated when I forget to call her.

Our world is in hurry

Most of the people don't really have time. The lack of time makes you to forget about things even more. The ridiculious situation is that sometimes people don't even have time to make to-do lists.

Creating a picture is usually more than enough. Pictures can remind you things which are very hard to express verbally.

Not long ago it happened to me that I had to run somewhere and in a hurry I just took a photo of my mother's picture to remind myself to call her.

A picture is worth 1000 words

Phodo allows you to create visual to-do lists, using photos you take, or photos sourced from the cloud.

Visual reminders provide better mental association than text-based to-do lists. Studies suggest that our brains can recognize an image in as little as 13 milliseconds. Compare that with the time it takes to read text-based items on a list, and there's no question which is more efficient.
